Our Story

Born from Clay,
Shaped by Passion

Redcocoon Pottery was born in a small sunlit studio, where the hum of the wheel and the warmth of the kiln became our daily rhythm. We believe that handmade ceramics carry a quiet energy — each piece holds the memory of the hands that shaped it.

Our work celebrates the beauty of natural materials and the slow, intentional process of creation. From sourcing local clays to developing our signature glazes, every step is guided by respect for the craft and love for the earth.

The Process

From Raw Earth
to Your Table

Every piece passes through patient hands and transformative fire. Here's how we bring clay to life.

01

Preparing the Clay

We begin by wedging and conditioning locally sourced clay, removing air bubbles and aligning the particles for a smooth, workable body.

02

Throwing & Shaping

On the potter's wheel or by hand, each form takes shape through a dialogue between the maker's intention and the clay's natural movement.

03

Glazing & Decoration

Our signature glazes are mixed in-house using natural minerals and oxides, applied by dipping, pouring, or brushwork for unique finishes.

04

Firing & Finishing

Each piece endures up to 1280°C in our kiln, transforming fragile clay into durable stoneware ready for a lifetime of daily use.
